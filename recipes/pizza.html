Cheese pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • pre-made pizza crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced tomatoes, basil leaves, red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended on the pizza crust package (usually around 425°F or 220°C).
  2. Prepare the crust: Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone. If using a store-bought crust, follow the instructions on the package for pre-baking, if required.
  3. Sauce it up: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Cheese it up: Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, covering the entire surface of the pizza.
  5. Add seasonings: Drizzle the olive oil over the cheese and sprinkle the dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Optional toppings: If desired, you can add sliced tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for extra flavor.
  7. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake according to the crust's instructions or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and slightly golden brown.
  8. Slice and serve: Once the pizza is done, carefully remove it from the oven. Allow it to cool for a minute or two before slicing it into desired portions.
  9. Enjoy: Serve your delicious homemade cheese pizza slices and enjoy!
