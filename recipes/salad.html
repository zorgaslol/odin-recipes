Caprese salad

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into rounds.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating them..
  3. Place a fresh basil leaf between each tomato and cheese slice.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salad.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing appetizer or side dish.
